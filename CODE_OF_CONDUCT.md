# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, technically honest, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent
- Focus critiques on code and behavior, not people
- Keep discussions evidence-based and specific
- Accept feedback and course-correct quickly

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warnings

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

## Scope

Applies to project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, chat) and related public interactions representing the project.
